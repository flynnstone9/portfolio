TOAST'D LONGBOARDS

This is a passion project. I prototyped multiple longboard builds and created “The Commute” longboard. The concept was developed during my daily commute from North Chicago to the Loop. Traveling to and from the “L” train was time consuming and wasteful. The goal of the board was to reduce this wasted time and allow riders to focus on what matters most. The branding is modern and simple, much like the board.

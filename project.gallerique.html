GALLERIQUE

Gallerique is an e-commerce start-up for painting, photography, sculpture, furniture, and antiques based out of Chicago. I was the tech lead at Gallerique. Managing our web designer and developer.

PROJECT 1

WORLD ART DUBAI 2016 x GALLERIQUE

Gallerique was commissioned by the World Art Dubai Artshow to handle their online presence. After receiving their project requirements, the Gallerique tech team was responsible for gathering images and information from all artist displaying work at the show. With their information, I created a page for each artist and all of their pieces. My other role was to design the art fair's web pages. This project had a tight deadline that our team met and was well received.

PROJECT 2

CONCEPT VIDEO INTRO / OUTRO

My first role at Gallerique was to produce videos to share with customers and artist to help illuminate Gallerique's brand. My objective was to make quick animations that were modern, minimal, and stylized.

GALLERIQUE

Gallerique is an e-commerce start-up for painting, photography, sculpture, furniture, and antiques based out of Chicago. I was the tech lead at Gallerique. Managing our web designer and developer. In addition, I helped onboard new artist onto the site. The skills I used at Gallerique inclulde HTML, CSS, JS, Google Suite, Photoshop, Illustrator, and After Effects.
